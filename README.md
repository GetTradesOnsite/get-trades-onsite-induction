# Get Trades Onsite – Induction

A QR-based, smartphone-first multilingual construction induction platform.

Workers scan a QR code, select their language, and follow live induction content in sync with the presenter.
Designed for safety, compliance, and audit-ready records.

## MVP Scope (Phase 1)
- QR session join (no login for workers)
- Language selection
- Live synced slide text
- “I understand” confirmation
- Compliance record capture

This is a regulated construction safety product.
The MVP prioritises clarity, reliability, and legal defensibility over polish.
